Origins起源 · qǐ yuán

Why six questions, and why these six?

The six questions trace back to Aristotle, who in his Nicomachean Ethics identified seven "circumstances of inquiry" — who, what, when, where, why, how, and by what means — as the elements every careful examination must consider before judging an action. Medieval scholastics adopted the list under the Latin mnemonic quis, quid, ubi, quibus auxiliis, cur, quomodo, quando, and used it to interrogate cases of conscience, criminal acts, and rhetorical claims. The framework outlived its theological origins and became the modern investigator's checklist — what journalists, courts, and consultants still use to make sense of any situation.

It was Rudyard Kipling who canonized the modern compressed form. In his 1902 poem from Just So Stories, he wrote: "I keep six honest serving-men. They taught me all I knew. Their names are What and Why and When and How and Where and Who." The 5W1H acronym took root from there, becoming the universal grammar of inquiry across journalism, project management, problem-solving, and now leadership development.

The DH Framework's contribution is the synthesis. The West asks; the East answers. 5W1H supplies the questions every development plan must answer — but the answers, generic to most frameworks, are differentiated by your BaZi chart. Your "Why" is named by the Driver in your reading. Your "What" is your Capability mapped to its actual element for your Daymaster. Your "Who, Where, When, How" inherit their elemental signatures from that one anchor. Two professionals reading the same self-help book end up with the same prescription. Two professionals running this framework — even with the same job and aspiration — end up with completely different IDPs because their charts are different. The questions are universal; the answers, finally, are yours.

05The Two Learning Modes

Sharpen the axe, or fell the tree?

Once Why and What are clear, How splits into two methodologies. Most people default to one without realizing — and that is where IDPs stall.

MODE 01 · P-ISLAND
Sharpening the Axe
Withdrawal for theoretical mastery.

Step back from the noise. Read, take a course, build mental models. P-Island rebuilds the foundation so the next swing is twice as effective.

Activities
  • Certifications, executive education, MBA modules
  • Deep reading, structured note-taking, journaling
  • Mental model construction and testing
  • Classroom learning, masterclasses, retreats
Triggered when your dominant Driver is
  • The Healer — needs rebalancing
  • The Visionary — designing a long arc
MODE 02 · Q-WAYS
Felling the Tree
Learning through real-world collision.

Learn by doing the thing, in public, with stakes. Q-Ways is the swing of the axe — messy, fast, the most direct path to competence you cannot fake.

Activities
  • Project execution with real consequences
  • "Fail fast" experiments and short cycles
  • Public performance, speaking, pitching
  • Competition, deadlines, visible outcomes
Triggered when your dominant Driver is
  • The Soldier — urgent real-world demand
  • The Surfer — riding existing momentum
  • The Ace — compounding natural talent

09Quick Glossary

Reference card.

Key terms you will encounter as you go deeper.

BaZi
"Eight Characters." Chinese metaphysical chart based on year, month, day, hour of birth.
Day Master
The Heavenly Stem of your Day Pillar. Represents you. Other elements are favorable or unfavorable relative to it.
Useful God
The element that brings your chart into balance — your remedy, your strategic advantage.
Luck Pillar
A 10-year energetic phase. Your "current decade" expressed in stem-branch terms.
Period 9
2024–2043. Feng Shui era favoring Fire industries — AI, media, technology, vision-based work.
Sheng Cycle
Generating cycle. Wood feeds Fire, Fire makes Earth, Earth produces Metal, Metal carries Water, Water nourishes Wood.
Ke Cycle
Controlling cycle. Wood breaks Earth, Earth dams Water, Water extinguishes Fire, Fire melts Metal, Metal cuts Wood.
Kong Wang
"Void." Energetic blanks in your chart. Often addressed via the Healer Driver and P-Island mode.
Life Stage
Crisis · Growth · Actualization · Expertise. Determines how the 5 Driver scores get weighted.
